ABOUT ORBITAL INTERNATIONAL

Established in 2002, Orbital International is a consulting firm with offices in Bangladesh and Canada. Our team of international associates work across multiple sectors, specializing in finance solutions for capital projects in Bangladesh. We also facilitate trade financing, private equity fundraising, and joint venture opportunities by connecting our clients with international lenders and investors. Our clients include some of the leading companies, commercial banks, and NGOs in Bangladesh.

We offer our clients an unparalleled combination of international finance expertise and extensive local knowledge. The strength of our company lies in the deep connections we have within Bangladesh’s business community, government and regulatory bodies, and partnerships with financial institutions around the world.
